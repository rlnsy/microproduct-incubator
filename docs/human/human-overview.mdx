## Layout Guide

The knowledge tree can be read like a textbook. If you move through the hub
sequentially, clicking through each next page in order, the structure will carry
you from the opening concepts into later chapters, one layer at a time.

That linear path is useful, but it is not the only way to read this hub. Some
readers will come looking for a specific module, topic, or author. Others may
have already read the full sequence and only want the newest or most relevant
parts as the hub grows.

For those readers, the tree becomes a web. Modules can point across branches to
recommended, related, or complementary material that may not appear next in the
sequential path. The tree gives the hub its structure; the web gives it movement,
letting people follow the knowledge they need instead of retracing every step.
