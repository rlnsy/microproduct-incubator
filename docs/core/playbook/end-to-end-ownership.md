## Phase Goal

Operate the microproduct as an outcome-focused product, not just a shipped codebase.

## Marketing Ownership

- Define the first distribution loops tied to a specific audience.
- Ship simple messaging that communicates problem, value, and proof.
- Track channel performance and double down on what converts.

## Advanced Insights Ownership

- Capture behavioral signals across activation, usage, and retention.
- Maintain a product learning backlog with evidence and confidence levels.
- Promote recurring insight reviews to guide roadmap decisions.

## Iteration Ownership

Use a repeatable cycle:

1. Hypothesis
2. Build
3. Measure
4. Decide

## Operating Rhythm After Launch

- Weekly: review metrics, user feedback, and top failures.
- Biweekly: prioritize experiments and scope the next iteration.
- Monthly: reassess positioning, market assumptions, and unit economics.

## Initial Module Scaffolding

- Marketing fundamentals for first distribution loops.
- Advanced insight framework: behavioral signals and product learning backlog.
- Iteration framework: hypothesis, build, measure, decide.
