# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to maintaining a welcoming, safe, and constructive community for all contributors.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and professional.
- Focus feedback on ideas and implementation, not people.
- Assume good intent and ask clarifying questions before escalating.
- Share actionable suggestions when requesting changes.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, personal attacks, or discriminatory language.
- Doxxing, intimidation, or threats.
- Repeated disruptive behavior after moderation requests.

## Enforcement

Committee members are responsible for enforcing this code of conduct.
They may remove content, restrict participation, or block contributors when necessary to protect the community.

## Reporting

To report behavior concerns, open a private issue or contact maintainers through the foundation channels.
